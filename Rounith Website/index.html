Cyber Congress

Cyber Congress 2022 aims at promoting and spreading mass awareness on various concepts under Cybersecurity. It aspires to introduce the fundamentals of Cybersecurity and promote awareness on the occasion of Cyber Awareness month. Its purpose is to provide high-profile, interactive and leading-edge events for students to exchange ideas, opinions, collaborate and learn from the learned ones. Cyber Congress also provides a platform for students to compete, where they can augment their skills and refine their knowledge in the key aspects of this domain. Broadly, the Congress with the theme “Cyber Safety and Cyber Security”, envisions promoting formal/non-formal sessions and competitions in the area of Cyber Security aimed at spreading mass awareness on Information/Cyber Security in the institution.
